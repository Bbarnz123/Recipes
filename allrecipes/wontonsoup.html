Wonton Soup

Introduction

This is a quick and reasonable recipe on how to make a wonton soup!

Ingredients

  • Wonton wrappers
  • Lean pork mince
  • Prawns
  • Ginger
  • Green onions
  • Light soy sauce
  • Chinese cooking wine
  • Salt
  • Sesame oil
  • Chicken broth
  • Garlic cloves
  • Shallots
  • Bok choy
  • egg noodles

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients (apart from noodles) to create filling. Wrap the filling.
  2. Cook or freeze for later!
  3. Use chicken broth for the broth itself.
  4. Prepare noodles for serving.
